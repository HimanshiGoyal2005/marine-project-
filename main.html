A team of researcher in united states and australia led by jenna jambeck,an environmental engineer at the university of georgia, analyzed plastic waste levels in the world's oceans.They found that china and indonesia is top source of plastic bottles,bags and other rubbish clogging up global sea lanes.Together,both nations account for more than third of plastic detritus in global waters,according to a report in the wall street journal.
